üéØ The Goal

I wanted to compare the two AI systems on:

  • System Usability ‚Äì How easy and pleasant the system is to use.

To measure these, I used two well-established UX questionnaires:

  • SUS ‚Äì System Usability Scale

    A short survey with 10 questions rated on a 1‚Äì5 scale (higher = better usability).

  • NASA-TLX ‚Äì Task Load Index

    A 6-question survey that assesses mental and physical workload. Originally on a 0‚Äì100 scale, I rescaled it to 1‚Äì5 for easier comparison.

üìâ Do the Scores Follow a Normal Distribution?

Many statistical tests assume data is normally distributed (bell-shaped curve).

I checked this using Shapiro-Wilk tests, which test whether scores differ significantly from normal.

  • All scores were non-normal (p < 0.001).
  • Therefore, I used non-parametric tests (Wilcoxon) that don‚Äôt require normality.
